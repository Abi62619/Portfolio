About Me

My special focus when it comes to game design is Games Programming. I really enjoy problem-solving that comes with writing scripts and I am fascinated with seeing the end product that can be created. This is why I decided to create my own portfolio website. By doing this I got to learn new programming languages HTML, CSS and a little bit of JavaScript. However, my main focus is in C# as this is the programming language that I use within Unity.


While I do love programming I have experience different areas of game design such as Level design, 3D modelling, Animation and creative writing. All parts of game developer interest me and this is why I want to go into the industry. The community that the industry creates is one of the main parts that drew into wanting to become a games programmer.


During my time at college I participated in multiple interviews for the colleges social media marketing. This interviews involved explain my Game Jam project on camera and showcasing my work for video through a playthrough. This along with working in team projects help me expand on my communication skills.
